# Capsule CRM: Feature Benchmark for POC

This document outlines the core features of Capsule CRM (as of 2026) to serve as a reference for our project's roadmap.

## 1. Core CRM & Contact Management
*   **Unified Contact View:** 360-degree timeline of interactions (emails, notes, files, tasks).
*   **Tags & DataTags:** Flexible categorization using tags and grouped custom fields.
*   **Relationship Intelligence:** Track "Milestones" and "Last Contacted" dates.

## 2. Sales Pipeline & Opportunities
*   **Visual Kanban Board:** Drag-and-drop interface for managing deals.
*   **Multiple Pipelines:** Support for different product/service sales workflows.
*   **Sales Forecasting:** Revenue prediction based on deal value and probability.

## 3. Task & Workflow Automation
*   **Tracks:** Sequences of tasks (e.g., "Onboarding Track") that can be applied to contacts.
*   **Calendar Sync:** Two-way sync with Google/Outlook.
*   **Repeating Tasks:** Automated reminders for recurring actions.

## 4. Post-Sale Project Management
*   **Projects Module:** Move beyond the sale. Convert "Opportunities" into "Projects" with their own stages and task lists.

## 5. AI & Modern Tools
*   **AI Content Assist:** Drafting and refining emails directly within the CRM.
*   **Inbox Add-ins:** Manage CRM data directly from Gmail/Outlook.

## 6. Integrations (Phase 2 Goal)
*   **Accounting:** Deep sync with Xero/QuickBooks for invoice visibility.
*   **Marketing:** Connection to email marketing platforms (Transpond/Mailchimp).

---

## Implications for our POC Roadmap
1.  **Phase 1 (Current):** Basic CRUD and CSV export.
2.  **Phase 2 (Next):** Implement "Interactions" (Timeline) and "Tags".
3.  **Phase 3:** Visual Sales Pipeline (Kanban) and basic "Tracks" (Task sequences).
4.  **Phase 4:** Simple Project management module.
